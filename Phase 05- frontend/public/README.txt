RAG Mutual Fund FAQ Chatbot - README
====================================

A facts-only mutual fund FAQ assistant. RAG-based chatbot that answers factual questions about five SBI mutual fund schemes using scraped public data. Every answer includes a citation link. No investment advice.

SCHEMES COVERED
---------------
- SBI US Specific Equity Active FoF Fund
- SBI Nifty Index Fund
- SBI Flexicap Fund
- SBI ELSS Tax Saver Fund
- SBI Large Cap Fund

Content is scraped from INDMoney mutual fund pages (public information only).

SAMPLE QUESTIONS
----------------
- What is the expense ratio of SBI ELSS Tax Saver Fund?
- How long is the lock-in period for SBI ELSS?
- Which benchmark does SBI Large Cap Fund follow?
- What is the current NAV of SBI Flexicap Fund?
- What is the minimum SIP amount for SBI Nifty Index Fund?

Questions that ask for advice (e.g. "Should I invest?") are refused with a polite, facts-only message.

DISCLAIMER
----------
This chatbot provides factual information from public sources only and does not provide investment advice. It does not recommend buying, selling, or holding any scheme. Use the cited sources for current details. Do not share PAN, Aadhaar, or other personal identifiers with the bot.
